What we categorise our captured images as:

Landscape Photography

Focuses on the beautiful scenery around you and freeze nature in one picture.

Wildlife Photography

Genre that deals with animals and their natural habitat and behaviours.

Portrait Photography

One that includes pics of family members to friends to pets.

Fashion Photography

Focuses on capturing models in glamourous light display fashion items.eg clothes, shoes and other accessories.

HDR Photography

One that blends in different types of pictures into a single one that highlights the amazing contrast of the individual images to create a true applealing look

Hi-Speed Photography

High speed is the one that captures images of events that take place in a rapid pace hence highlight the beauty of fast phenomenon one at a time.

Moblie Photography

One of the most popular types of photography especially in the view of the fact that mobile phones cameras today offer the precision and clarity of professional equipment.

Modelling Photography

Focuses on pictures of professional models for promotion of various products and services mostly useful in business orgainization.

Nature Photography

Such pictures are more of the outdoor nature as water bodies, falls etc.

Night Photography

Night not only offers an entirely different perspective of things but also provides great depths of colors and further enhances the magic and mystery of clicking images at night
